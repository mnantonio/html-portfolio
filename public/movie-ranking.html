The Best Movies According to Marco

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Eternal Sunshine for the Spotless Mind

When their relationship turns sour, a couple undergoes a medical procedure to have each other erased from their memories for ever. Great Sci-Fi Rom-Com, the chemistry between Kate Winslet and Jim Carrey is very observable.

Joe Vs. The Volcano

When a hypochondriac learns that he is dying, he accepts an offer to throw himself in a volcano at a tropical island, and along the way there, learns to truly live. It has a lot of symbolism and deeper meaning into it. Meg Ryan plays three different characters in this movie.

Toy Story

A cowboy doll is profoundly threatened and jealous when a new spaceman action figure supplants him as top toy in a boy's bedroom. The first computer animated movie released. Wonderful story for the whole family.
